Writings

The Windows into my World

Welcome to my Scribble Corner!

Here you'll find written records of my explorations and adventures, whether within the field of data science or out there in the big wide world! Whatever I watch, read, build and experience, is more valuable than just something to be passively consumed. Each of these has a specialty, a point of view, or even a lesson, and writing helps me to uncover it.

Writing is an active processs that helps me separate the important from the inconsequential, organize my thoughts and express myself clearly. It encourages reflection and clarity, making me a better thinker, observer and communicator.

Most importantly, writing acts like a camera that catches hold of fleeting sparks of insight and lessons learnt, assimilates them as tangible snapshots and preserves them for looking back on in the future. Take a look at my picture album!
